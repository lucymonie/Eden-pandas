Giant Pandas

Giant pandas live in China

They mostly live in Sichuan, Shaanxi and Gansu, which are in central China.

Habitat

Giant pandas live in the mountains because people kept on cutting down the trees in the places where they live and now they have to live in the mountains.

The people who cut down the places wanted to grow wood and rice for their families.

Population

There are only one thousand and six hundred giant pandas left in the wild and in zoos there are three hundred more.

Giant pandas eat bamboo. Pandas are actually omnivores but their very favourite food is bamboo.

Dangers

Giant pandas are endangered because snow leopards eat them, and because farmers take the place that they used to live.

Giant pandas are being protected from people by making sure that the pandas have somewhere to live and people have places to grow their rice.
